Your Host

Tom Carr — mortgage broker, former banker, and your guide through the property web.

After 20 years inside the big banks in London and Sydney, I crossed the floor to work for clients instead of lenders. The Property Web is a natural extension of that — because getting to the right property decision takes more than a mortgage broker. It takes a whole network.

Each episode, I sit down with one of those people — buyer's agents, accountants, lawyers, planners — to give you an honest window into how they think, what they cost, and whether they're the right fit for your situation.

Think of it as doing your research, but in conversation form.
