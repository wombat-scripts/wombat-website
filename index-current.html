Why Work With Me

Not your typical broker.
Not even close.

After two decades inside the big banks — in London and Sydney — I crossed the floor to work for clients, not lenders. I know exactly how banks think, what they'll move on, and where the levers are.

01
Institutional experience, boutique service

Two decades inside the big banks gives you insight you can't buy from a course. I know how lenders assess risk, what they'll negotiate on, and how to position your application to win.

02
A specialist in situations others call "too hard"

Foreign income. Temporary visas. Multiple currencies. Complex structures. If a bank has said no, that's usually a lender-fit problem — not a you problem. This is exactly where I thrive.

03
Strategy before product — always

I don't lead with rates. I lead with your situation. Once we understand your goals, timeline and risk appetite, the right product becomes obvious. You'll leave our first call with clarity, not confusion.

04
A thinking partner, not a form-filler

My job isn't to process your paperwork. It's to help you make a decision you'll feel great about in ten years. That means honest conversations, real numbers, and zero banker-speak.
