I've sat on both sides of the desk. That's the short version of why this works.

Two decades of my career were spent in senior data and analytics roles at Citi, UBS and RBS in London, then Commonwealth Bank in Sydney. I know how lenders think because I've been the lender. I know what their credit models actually look at, what they care about, and — just as usefully — what they quietly ignore.

I also know what it feels like to be the foreigner with a complicated application. I moved from the UK to Sydney fifteen years ago. I've been the expat trying to translate a payslip in a different currency, the new arrival wondering whether the bank really meant 'no' or just hadn't bothered to look properly.

In June 2024, I left the corporate world to start Wombat Home Loans. The name comes from the Beecroft Wombats — the kids' soccer team I coach. Wombats are steady, friendly, and stubbornly determined. That's the company I wanted to build: no jargon, no sales theatrics, and the persistence to actually get you over the line.

— Tom

Today, I work with first-home buyers, expats, international professionals and investors all over Australia. I have access to 40+ lenders and, more importantly, a working memory of which lender will actually look kindly on your specific situation. That's the value of having spent 20 years inside the machine.

Who I Help

If your story doesn't fit a five-question online form, you're in the right place.

Most of my clients come to me after they've been told no, or been quoted something that didn't add up. Here's where I tend to add the most value.

First home buyers

A clear, realistic path to ownership in Sydney — from how much you can actually borrow, through grants and schemes, to settlement day.

Expats & international professionals

Foreign income, FIRB rules, unfamiliar credit history. Lenders treat these very differently — I know which ones say yes.

Property investors

Structuring borrowing across multiple properties, refinancing for equity release, and avoiding the common tax-time landmines.

Refinancing

If you haven't reviewed your loan in 18+ months, there's a strong chance you're leaving money on the table. Let's check.

Self-employed & complex income

Contractors, business owners, bonus-heavy comp, equity grants. The bank calculator says no — the right lender says yes.

Cross-border & complex residency

PR pending, dual residency, partner offshore. I work with clients whose situations require an actual conversation, not a tickbox.

How It Works

Four conversations between us and your keys.

No pressure, no jargon, no surprises. The process is genuinely simple once someone walks you through it.

01

Discovery call

A 30-minute, no-cost chat. Tell me your situation. I tell you what I see, honestly. No paperwork yet.

02

Strategy

I map your full picture — income, goals, timeline, lender appetite — and come back with a clear plan.

03

Application

I do the heavy lifting. Forms, lender liaison, follow-ups. You sign things; I chase everything else.

04

Settlement & beyond

I don't disappear at settlement. Annual reviews, life changes, refinancing — I'm still your broker.
